    My education from primary school, secondary school and university levels.

     

    My education during primary school was at Sekolah Kebangsaan Permai Indah, Bukit Mertajam, Pulau Pinang. There, I went from grade one to grade six. This school is near my home. So I could just go to school by riding the bike that my dad bought. During primary school, I was very active in joining clubs or associations. In sixth grade, I successfully passed the Ujian Pencapaian Sekolah Rendah (UPSR) exam with 2A 3B results.


     

    My education during secondary school was at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Permai Indah, Bukit Mertajam, Pulau Pinang. There, I went from form one to form five. This school is very nearest from my home. So I could just go to school by walked that just take 5 minutes. During primary school, I was very active in joining clubs or associations. In form three and form five, I successfully passed the Pentaksiran Tingkatan 3 (PT3) exam and the Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) exam with excellent results.



    Then, upon ended from secondary school, I was able to continue my university studies by applying for a course Diploma in Office Management and Technology in UiTM Dungun, Terengganu. The course took me about two and a half years from 2018 to 2020. I can't forget when I got a dean for three consecutive semesters and it made me even more excited to continue studying at a higher level.


    After completing my diploma course at UiTM Dungun, Terengganu, I am currently pursuing a degree in Bachelor in Office Systems Management UiTM Machang, Kelantan. I am currently in the second semester of the February - July session. For the final semester, I will attend industry training and I will do my best as I can. I hope my journey as a degree student are going well.
